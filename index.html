Welcome to The People’s Rules of Live TV.

But before you take a look at the rules that help make live TV more enjoyable, don’t forget the rule that makes it legal. Because, whatever live TV you enjoy – and no matter what channel, streaming service or device you’re watching on – you need to be covered by a TV Licence. It also allows you to watch BBC Programmes on iPlayer.

So, if you don’t already have a TV Licence, buy yours now.
